Words by Tim Rice, music by Freddie Mercury and Mike Moran

Each dash (-) or chord represents a crotchet (except for a couple of
places where shorter rhythms are used, but you'll know what to do),
and repeated dashes mean that the preceding chord is repeated.

Sometimes I've represented the overall harmony rather than just
the piano part, but it all sounds good to me.
